```python

f.write("test")
f.close()
```

- `print`を使った記述
```python 
f = open('test.txt', 'w')
print('I am print', file=f)
f.close()
```

- `print`記述なので、好きなように記述可能
```python
f = open('test.txt', 'w')
name, family = 'Mike', 'David'
print(f'I am {name} {family}', end='!!!', file=f)
f.close()
```
